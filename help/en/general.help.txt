?help

Information about bot commands.

Aliases
  ?h
  ?command
  ?cmd

Usages
  ?help [--general]
  ?help <--admin|--owner>
  ?help <command-name>

Options
  --general       List all general commands
  --admin         List all guild administrator commands
  --owner, --dev  List all bot owner commands

Examples
  ?help --general
  ?help ping
